Avocado

The avocado (Persea americana) is a medium-sized, evergreen
tree in the laurel family (Lauraceae). It is native to the
Americas and was first domesticated in Mesoamerica more
than 5,000 years ago.Then as now it was prized for its
large and unusually oily fruit.
